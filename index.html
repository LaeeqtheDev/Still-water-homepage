Everything you need to know about Stillwater

Is Stillwater really free?

+

Yes! Stillwater is 100% free, forever. No hidden costs, no premium tiers, no ads. We believe mental health tools should be accessible to everyone.

How is my data protected?

+

We use end-to-end encryption for all your data. Your journal entries, mood data, and personal information are encrypted before leaving your device. We can't read them, and neither can anyone else.

Do you sell my data?

+

Never. We don't sell, share, or monetize your data in any way. No ads, no tracking, no third-party analytics. Your privacy is sacred to us.

Can I use it offline?

+

Yes! Most features work offline including journaling, mood tracking, and viewing your past data. Meditation and AI chat require an internet connection.

Is this a replacement for therapy?

+

No. Stillwater is a wellness tool, not a medical device. It's designed to support your mental health journey, but it doesn't replace professional therapy or medical treatment.

When is iOS version coming?

+

We're working on it! iOS version is in development and will launch in Q3 2026. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when it's ready.
